About Shelfy

Built to solve a real problem.

Shelfy started with a simple observation — people don't waste food because they don't care. They waste it because once the fridge door closes, it disappears from their brain. Out of sight, out of mind.

We spent weeks talking to hundreds of people about their food habits. The same story came up over and over — good intentions at the shops, forgotten food at home, guilt when it ended up in the bin. People with ADHD said it especially felt impossible to keep track.

So we built Shelfy. A simple, fast, friendly app that does the remembering for you. Scan your food when you get home, and we'll remind you before it expires — with recipe ideas so nothing goes to waste.

Shelfy is free to use and built with care in the UK.
